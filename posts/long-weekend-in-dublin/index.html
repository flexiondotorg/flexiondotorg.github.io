Long weekend in Dublin

Dublin Ireland

We just got back from an enjoyable trip to Dublin, highlights for me were visits to the…

  • Guinness Storehouse for a history lesson and a drop of the black stuff.
  • The Old Jameson Distillery for another history lesson and a drop of the hard stuff, I also certified as an Irish Whiskey taster during the visit. I have a certificate and everything :-)
  • Gallagher’s Boxty House for some traditional Irish grub.
  • Ryanair’s ticket prices, just 10p return each!

This was our first visit to Ireland and I am looking forward to going back again soon and maybe incorporate some diving.
